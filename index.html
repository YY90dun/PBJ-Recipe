PB&J Recipe!

Learn how to make a ultra-simple Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • High Quality Grape Jelly

Instructions

  1. Lay out 2 slices of bread.
  2. Spread peanut putter on a slice.
  3. Spread jelly on top of the peanut butter.
  4. Stick the 2 slices together with the "goober" on the inside
